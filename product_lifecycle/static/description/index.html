Product Lifecycle

Replacement a Discontinued Product

This module apply a part of the product lifecycle management concept by extending the logic of the field Status defined in the Odoo base modules and by adding a list of repaclement products that will be used as alternatives in purchase operations when a product is discontinued.

Odoo manage a informative status field in the Product Template when you can know the product liecycle status. This module hide this not used functionality and used a similar status field in the Product Variant. This way we can differenciate when a specific variant is discontinued and what other products are possible replacements.

Lifecycle Status

You can check the status of Products Variants form the Product Template form view by checking the Status section in the Inventory Tab. You will find a list of the Products Variants and its current status.

The status of the Product Variant is manage in the header of form view as a clickable statusbar widget and with default value In Development.

Also some search filters were added to search and group by products lifecycle status. Just go to a Product Variant list view and filter.

Replacement Products

A new field named Replacement Products was added to the Product Variant to hold a list of products that can be use as a replacement in purchase operations when a product is discontinued.

This module restrict the purchase orders to can not have discontinued products. When creating or editing a purchase order you will see a new field named Discontinued Product at the order lines. This field register when a replacement of a discontinued product have been made. The line show the information of the replaced product (discontinued) and the replacement product.

If you fill Discontinued Product field the Product field will be filter with the good replacements products (active and not obsolete products marked as a replacements of the current discontinued product). In other case, if the user try to fill the Product field with a discontinued product will show a warning indicating that can not use a discontinued product in the purchase order and will move this value to the Discontinued Product field and clean the Product field so you can select a replacement.

Add a new functionality that can be access from the Check Discontinued Products button in the purchase order form. This will let to check if a purchase order has discontinued product and will let to update the purchase order lines with a replacement of the discontinued products configured at the product form.

Note: This wizard works in the case that a purchase order was created and then one o more products in the order lines have been discontinued.

The Purchase order will be updated with the new replacements selected: The replacement product will be put in the Product field and the discontinued product will be move to the Discontinued Product field.

Product Edit Restriction

A new access rules over the Products and Product Variants records were added. A product only can be edited by his product manager.
