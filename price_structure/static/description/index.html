Price Structure

This module adds a new functionality to manage price lists for products adding a new tab on the product view where you can see the price lists where this product is affected either by product or by category.

It also adds a new feature to the time of a sale, allowing you to choose a price list through which Live will calculate to give a product price depending on the price list q be selected, regardless of whether the list price selected at the beginning of the order.

This also adds a blockade preventing sold when selling a product at a price higher or lower than estimated by their price lists
